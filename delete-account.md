# Delete Your MIOS-X Clinical AI Account

Users may request deletion of their MIOS-X Clinical AI account and associated account data.

## How to request deletion

Send an account deletion request using the support contact shown on the MIOS-X Clinical AI Google Play Store listing.

Include:

- Your Google account email used to sign in
- The subject: MIOS-X Account Deletion Request
- A clear request to delete your MIOS-X account data

## Data that may be deleted

Where applicable, the following account-related data may be deleted:

- Account profile information associated with Google Sign-In
- MIOS-X account preferences stored by the app or connected services
- Subscription entitlement information stored by MIOS-X systems

## Data that may be retained

Some transaction or subscription records may be retained by Google Play or other service providers according to legal, accounting, fraud-prevention, or platform requirements.

Deleting your MIOS-X account does not automatically cancel an active Google Play subscription. You should manage or cancel subscriptions through Google Play.

## Processing time

Account deletion requests will be reviewed and processed within a reasonable period after identity verification.
